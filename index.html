About Me

Welcome to my personal homepage! I obtained both my master's and Ph.D. degrees from University of California, Davis, and my graduate research was focused on using multi-fidelity CFD to study low-noise solutions for wind turbines. Now, I am an aeroacoustics engineer based in the Bay Area. I work for Lucid Motors to make electric cars more aerodynamically efficient and quieter. Not only do I perform Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) simulations but also wind tunnel and road tests. I am dedicated to advance environment-friendly technologies, and hopefully, make our planet a safe, clean place to live. Prior to joining Lucid, I interned at Dassault Systems as a software engineer to develop their cutting-edge CFD software PowerFLOW.
